Kristu Jayanti (Deemed to be University)

Established in 1999 and managed by the Bodhi Niketan Trust of the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI), was reaccredited with an 'A++' grade by NAAC in 2021. Recognized by UGC under category 3 and granted Deemed to be University status since 2025, the university is committed to academic excellence, values, and global competencies. In 2024, it was ranked 60th in the NIRF top 100 colleges in India. Internationally, its School of Management holds ACBSP accreditation, and it has DBT Star College status from the Ministry of Science & Technology. The institution has also won national accolades, including the Clean and Smart Campus Award, and ranks highly across various disciplines in the India Today - MDRA 2025 survey, distinguishing itself among the best colleges in Bengaluru and nationally.

Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science has been an integral part of the college since its inception in 1999. Offering contemporary programmes in Computer Science, including BCA Analytics, Cloud Computing, Cyber Security, BSc AI/ML, IOT, and Data Science, The department strives to prepare students for both career prospects and continued academic pursuits within the field. The department has also been ranked 4th Best in BCA among colleges in India at the India Today MDRA Survey. The department fosters continuous knowledge and skill enhancement by encouraging participation in conferences, seminars, workshops, and by facilitating the publication of research articles. A distinctive feature of the department is its academic alliance with various industries to bridge the gap between industry and academia. Industry partners such as Celonis, QLIK, UiPath, SalesForce, Oracle Academy, Microsoft Dynamics Academic, ICT Academy, ACM-W, NPTEL, AWS Academy, Dell EMC, Palo Alto, Honeywell, SAP, Infosys, and VMware IT Academy collaborate with us to craft the curriculum addressing contemporary skill gaps in novel computing technologies. These alliances ensure that students receive practical, up-to-date training that aligns with industry standards, enhancing their employability and competitiveness in the job market.

University of Oldenburg, Germany

The University of Oldenburg, founded in 1973, is one of Germany’s leading young public research universities, located in Lower Saxony. It is renowned for its interdisciplinary approach and strong focus on sustainability, renewable energy, neurosciences, education, and information technology. The university offers a wide range of bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral programs, emphasizing research-led teaching and international collaboration.

Murray State University, USA

Murray State University, established in 1922 in Kentucky, USA, is a nationally recognized public university known for academic excellence and student-centered learning. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs, with strong emphasis on computer science, business, education, and health sciences. Committed to experiential learning and global engagement, Murray State fosters innovation through research, international partnerships, and hands-on learning opportunities. With a diverse and inclusive campus environment, the university prepares students to thrive in a global society.
