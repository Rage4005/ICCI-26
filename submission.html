Submission

Submission Guidelines

  • Submitted research papers must be original and not previously published elsewhere.
  • All accepted and presented papers will be submitted for the possible publication in Scopus / Conference Proceedings.
  • Authors will be responsible for issues related to plagiarism (Must be less than 10%).
  • Articles should be submitted only through Mail (ncci@kristujayanti.com).
  • Only the research articles presented during the conference will be considered for publication.
  • Registration will be confirmed only after the payment of registration fee.
  • Paper submission format (IRJMT): Click here
  • Selected good quality papers will be published in peer reviewed international journal -Kristu Jayanti Journal of Computational Sciences (ISSN: 2583-6072) with doi and free of cost

Instructions for Authors

  • The Papers should be based on the original work and not yet published.
  • Paper Submission can be done through Easy Chair conference management system.
  • Acceptance of papers for presentation / publication will be intimated to the authors via email.
  • Registration will be confirmed only after payment of registration fee.
  • Research papers accepted and presented during the conference will be considered for publication.

General Guidelines

  • Certificates will be issued only for registered participants attending the conference on both days.
  • Accommodation can be arranged on prior request with associated fees applicable.
