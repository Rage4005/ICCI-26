International Conference on Computational Intelligence - ICCI 26'

Fusion of Computational Mastery and Intelligence to Explore New Realms for Real-World Solution

Important Announcements

Conference date: February 27th and 28th, 2025.

ICCI Preamble

The Department announces the International Conference on Computational Intelligence (ICCI), scheduled for January 29th and 30th, 2026. The conference focuses on the theme – “Fusion of Computational Mastery and Intelligence to Explore New Realms for Real-World Solution”. This conference will serve as a platform to delve into the latest developments, approaches, research trends, challenges, and unresolved issues in Computational Intelligence. ICCI-2026 aims to bring together distinguished Academicians, Researchers, Engineers, and Scientists from across the country to share their insights and present new advances and research outcomes in the field. Through this exchange of knowledge, the conference endeavours to narrow the gap between academic research and practical applications in Computational Intelligence. ICCI-2026 will delve into the societal and commercial implications of Computational Intelligence, addressing its impact on diverse aspects of life, economy, and the environment.
